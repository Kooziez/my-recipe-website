Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Recipe Information

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 2-4

This recipe is quick, easy, and flavorful. It is visually appealing and will make you crave for more!

Ingredients

  • 8oz Fettuccine Noodles
  • 1 pound Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 tablespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • 1 teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 cups Arugula or Spinanh
  • 5oz Cherry Tomatoes, cut in half
  • Salt and Black Pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons Parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Season shimp with salt and pepper, to taste, and 1/2 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes; set aside.
  3. In a large saute pan or cast iron skillet, melt 2 tablespoons butter over medium high heat. Add the garlic, oregano, and the rest of the crushed red pepper flakes, and cook stirring frequently, until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add shrimp and cook, stirring occasionally, until the shrimp turns pink, about 2-3 minutes; set aside.
  5. Melt the remaining 6 tablespoons of butter in the pan or skillet. Then stir in pasta, arugula or spinanch, cherry tomatoes, and parmesan until the arugula or spinach begins to wilt; about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add shimp and toss to combine.
  7. Serve immediately, garnish with parsley, if desired.

Tips

Adjust the spice level by varying the amount of red pepper flakes.

Be sure not to over cook the shrimp as it will become tough.

If your shrimp is frozen, you can thaw them quickly by running them under cold water; about 5-10 minutes
